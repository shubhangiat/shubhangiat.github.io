Shubhangi Agarwal

I am a researcher with experience in Graph databases and Graph mining. I did my post-doctoral research with Prof. Angela Bonifati at the LIRIS lab in University Lyon 1, France. We explored the possibility of a novel hybrid data model that combines the properties of both Property graphs and Time series, for a time-enriched graph representation.

I got my PhD in 2023 from Department of Computer Science, IIT Kanpur, India, where I worked with my supervisor and mentor Prof. Arnab Bhattacharya. My thesis focused on Approximate Subgraph Querying in both deterministic and probabilistic settings, as well as explored node embeddings in graphs based on relative position of the nodes in the graph.
